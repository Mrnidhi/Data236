The Artist

Behind the Canvas

I'm Liberty, a visual artist based in the vibrant creative community of San Francisco. My work is an ongoing investigation into human emotion—capturing fleeting feelings in permanent strokes of color.

With a background in traditional oil painting and a passion for modern digital mediums, I bridge the gap between old-world technique and new-age expression. My studio is my sanctuary, a place where messy experiments turn into finished stories.
